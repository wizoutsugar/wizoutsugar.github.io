Introduction To Red Team

2021-10-31 — Written by WizoutSugar
#redteam 
1. Red Team The purpose of a red team is to simulate a genuine, real-life threat to an organisation to improve people, processes and technology. They’re given a specific goal: Demonstrating access to business-critical information or measure blue teams' detection and response policies and will identify gaps in the organisation’s defences, monitoring and incident response capabilities to reach that goal. Red Team Exercises focus on an end-to-end assessment of the entire organization.
